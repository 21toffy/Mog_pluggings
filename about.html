Our Story

Founded in 2020, Mog Pluggings emerged from a simple observation: the traditional recruitment model was broken. Companies were spending months trying to hire engineers, while talented developers struggled to find roles that valued their skills.

We set out to build a better way. By combining proprietary AI matching technology with rigorous human vetting, we created a platform that removes the friction from hiring.

Today, we partner with over 500 companies globally, from seed-stage startups to Fortune 500 enterprises, helping them build diverse, high-performing teams.
