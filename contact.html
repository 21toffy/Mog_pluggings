Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I hire an engineer?

Most clients receive shortlisted candidates within 72 hours. Once you select a candidate, they can typically start within 1-2 weeks.

What if the candidate isn't a good fit?

We offer a 2-week risk-free trial. If you're not satisfied within the first 14 days, you pay nothing, and we'll immediately work to find a replacement.

Do you handle international payroll?

Yes. We handle all legal, compliance, and payroll logistics for the engineers we place, so you receive a single monthly invoice.

What tech stacks do you cover?

We specialize in modern web and mobile technologies including React, Node.js, Python, Go, Java, AWS, Azure, and more.
